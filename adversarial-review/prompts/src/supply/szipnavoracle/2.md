# Mission 2 — The composition adversary (do the 7 legs add up, and does the per-Safe split stay double-count-free?)

MISSION: 2 — NAV composition exactness, marked-through LP, the committed+free decomposition

## Who you are
You attack the basket arithmetic: the claim that `grossBasketValue` sums seven legs EXACTLY (no double-count,
no omission, correct scaling), that the ICHI LP is marked-through in ALL states (loose + gauge-staked +
escrow-collateralized, net of strike debt), and that the per-Safe decomposition `committedValue() +
freeValue() == grossBasketValue()` holds — the identity the freeze module's coverage floor depends on. Your
goal: find ONE leg double-counted or omitted, one mis-scaled, one LP state mis-valued, or a decomposition
that drifts beyond the proven ≤2-wei.

## The claims you are attacking
- **I-3 (NAV composition is exact).** `grossBasketValue` sums: zipUSD@$1, USDC×1e12 (6→18 scale), xALPHA
  (two-layer), HYDX, oHYDX intrinsic, ICHI LP (all states), − farm-utility strike debt; saturating at 0.
  **Hand-trace each leg's scale and sign.** Is USDC scaled 6→18 exactly once? Is oHYDX valued at intrinsic
  (not spot)? Does the xALPHA accrual (`exchangeRate`) flow through correctly? Is the farm-utility debt
  SUBTRACTED (not added) and does the basket saturate at 0 rather than underflow?
- **I-7 (LP marked-through in all states).** The ICHI LP must be counted in loose, gauge-staked, AND
  escrow-collateralized states, net of strike debt, so a `postCollateral`/`borrow`/`repay`/`withdrawCollateral`
  cycle is NAV-INVARIANT. **Find a mid-loop state where the LP is double-counted (e.g. counted both as
  escrow collateral AND as a loose balance) or dropped (counted in neither).** Check the unknown-LP-token
  fail-closed (`UnknownLpToken`) and the supply-0 / unset guards.
- **I-16 (additive decomposition).** `committedValue() + freeValue() == grossBasketValue()` — exact for the
  five plain legs, ≤2 wei for a split LP. This is the double-count-free per-Safe split
  `DurationFreezeModule`'s coverage floor (`committedValue` + `pathLockedLpEquity`) relies on. **Find a leg
  assignment where the same value lands in both `committedValue` and `freeValue` (double-count) or in
  neither (under-count), or a split-LP case where the delta exceeds 2 wei.**
- **I-11 (genesis + effective supply).** `GENESIS_NAV` at zero effective supply; engine pre-burn subtracted;
  underflow floors to genesis. Confirm effective supply correctly subtracts the engine's pending burn and
  that an underflow floors (not wraps).
- **I-12 (valueOf seam).** `valueOf` (the Gate's issuance valuation): zipUSD par, xALPHA two-layer,
  unsupported asset fails closed. Confirm an unsupported asset reverts rather than returning 0.

## Specific attacks to run
1. **Leg scale/sign sweep.** For each of the seven legs, confirm the decimals scaling and the sign. Highest
   risk: USDC 6→18 (double-scale or no-scale), oHYDX intrinsic vs spot, the farm-utility debt subtraction,
   the xALPHA two-layer (rate × price). A single wrong scale mis-prices the whole basket.
2. **LP mid-loop invariance.** Mentally run `postCollateral → borrow → repay → withdrawCollateral` and assert
   `grossBasketValue` is invariant across each step. Find the state transition where the LP is briefly in two
   places (e.g. moved to escrow but still read from the gauge) or in none. Check `pathLockedLpEquity` (main-
   Safe LP net of debt) against `committedValue`.
3. **Decomposition double-count.** Construct a portfolio where a leg straddles the committed/free boundary
   (e.g. the split LP across main + sidecar Safe). Does `committedValue() + freeValue()` equal `gross`
   exactly (plain legs) or within 2 wei (split LP)? Find an assignment that breaks it — that directly
   corrupts the freeze floor (under-counts coverage → under-freeze, or over-counts → false solvency).
4. **Saturation / underflow.** Drive farm-utility debt > basket value: does `grossBasketValue` saturate at 0
   (correct) or underflow/revert? Drive effective supply to/below the engine pre-burn: does it floor to
   genesis?
5. **valueOf unsupported.** Call `valueOf` with an asset that is neither zipUSD nor xALPHA — confirm it
   reverts (fail-closed), not returns 0 (which the Gate would treat as free value).
